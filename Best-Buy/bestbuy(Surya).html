Product Overview

Samsung washing machines are designed for quick cycles and simple use, making them ideal for busy families. This front loader from Samsung uses unique, high-quality components and features to help get your clothes clean with less time and effort.

To keep power and water bills manageable and your clothes in top-notch condition, this Samsung top loader uses Bubble Wash technology to simulate the effects of a hot wash while using cold water. By dissolving detergent with air and water from the beginning of the washing cycle, the machine cushions your delicate and synthetic fabrics during the wash.

Busy families that need fresh clothes in a hurry will love the 15-minute Quick Wash setting. Your favourite clothes can be cleaned quickly and conveniently, so you can be ready and out the door sooner.
